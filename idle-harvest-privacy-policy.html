By downloading or using Idle Harvest, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. We do not sell your personal information. This policy should be read together with our Terms of Service. Terms used here have the same meaning as there unless otherwise defined.



1. Information We Collect

Information Collected Automatically

When you use the game, we (and our service providers) may automatically collect:

  • Device information: device type, operating system and version, unique device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID), language, and time zone.
  • Usage data: how you use the app (e.g., features used, session length, in-app events) to improve the game and analyze performance.
  • Crash and performance data: information about crashes, errors, and performance issues to fix bugs and improve stability.
  • Approximate location: may be inferred from IP address (e.g., country or region) for analytics and ad serving; we do not collect precise location (e.g., GPS) unless you explicitly grant it.

Information You Provide

We do not require you to create an account or provide your name, email, or other contact details to play. If you contact us for support or feedback (e.g., by email), we will use the information you provide only to respond to you. We do not use it for marketing unless you consent.

No Sensitive or Adult Content

Idle Harvest does not contain adult content. We do not collect sensitive categories of data (such as health, financial, or precise location) for the purpose of the game. We do not support in-app sharing of user-generated content to other users or to social networks; any data stays on your device or is processed only as described in this policy.



2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the game.
  • Understand how the game is used (analytics) and fix crashes and errors (crash reporting).
  • Deliver and measure advertisements (including personalized ads where permitted and where you have not opted out).
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.



3. Third-Party Services

We use services provided by third parties to run analytics, crash reporting, and advertising. Each has its own privacy practices. We encourage you to review their policies.

Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics)

We use Firebase, a service provided by Google LLC, for:

  • Firebase Analytics: to understand how the app is used (e.g., screens viewed, events). This may include device identifiers, IP address, and usage data.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: to collect crash reports and diagnostic information so we can fix bugs and improve stability.

Data collected by Firebase is processed according to Google's Privacy Policy. You can learn more about Firebase and privacy at Firebase Privacy. You may be able to limit or opt out of certain Google analytics and advertising features via your device settings (e.g., "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS, "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android) or through Google's tools.

Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to show advertisements in the game. AdMob may collect and use data such as device identifiers (including the advertising ID), IP address, and app usage to deliver and measure ads, including personalized ads when allowed by your settings and applicable law.

You can control ad personalization and limit tracking via your device settings (e.g., "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS, "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android). For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob and user privacy.
